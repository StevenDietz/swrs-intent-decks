The DSO advantage

Why fresh invoices recover best

A commercial balance is most collectible while it’s fresh; every month it ages, contact data goes stale and leverage erodes.

The age curve

Fresh accounts (under ~90 days) often resolve in 30–60 days; balances that sit a year collect a fraction of that.

The standing rule

A fixed “place at 90 days” policy recovers materially more than letting balances drift — and on contingency there’s no cost to placing.
